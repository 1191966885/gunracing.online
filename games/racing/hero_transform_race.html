Game Description

Hero Transform Race is an action-packed and fun parkour game that transforms you from an ordinary person into a legendary superhero. Your goal is to reach the end of the track by transforming into the appropriate superhero form for the obstacles you encounter!

Instructions

You can use "MOUSE" or touch controls to change heroes.
For 2 player mode, you can use "A,S,D" and "J,K,L" keys for shortcuts to change heroes.
