Game Description

Cool collection of dynamic action games! Fight with rotating weapons, score goals in football, Take first place in a long race, Complete difficult survival levels and much more. There is a quick round for a couple of minutes and a tournament for several rounds. You can play up to 4 players, play with friends and bots. You can change the player's appearance: change cars, faces, color. Play and have a lot of fun!

Instructions

The controls are simple and different for each mode. They are shown before each round.

In each mode you can find crystals, they are needed to buy new cars, faces and colors.

Each completed round is one key. Keys are given out after the winner is announced. They are needed to open new modes.
