Geometrix

Adventure Platformer

Game Description

Geometrix is a game about guessing the right shape. Defeat the enemies copying their shapes. When you collide with the evil shapes, if you have the same geometrical figure, you will destroy the enemy, otherwise you will lost a health point. Lost more than three points and it will be game over. Face over 40 levels, with increasing difficulty, enemies with up to 6 different geometric figures. You have to be fast and skilled to beat this game. Will you make it? If you want to know it, just try it!

Instructions

Touch the buttons on screen to change shape
