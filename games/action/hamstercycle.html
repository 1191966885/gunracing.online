Game Description

Help a genius hamster bust out of a mad professor's lab on his slick DIY ride - the Hamstercycle! Jump across furniture and whatever junk the prof left lying around to reach the portal at the end of each room. But watch out - the floor is guarded by laser sensors! Touch it, and the hamster gets caught! Fun physics, simple controls, and hundreds of exciting levels await!

Instructions

In the main menu, press "Play" or the Spacebar to start a level.
Tap the screen, click anywhere, or press Space to jump.
To exit a level and return to the menu, click the house icon in the top-right corner.
