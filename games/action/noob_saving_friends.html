Game Description

"Noob: Saving friends" - An adventure in an interesting and captivating world with Nubik. Together with Nubik, you will mine and sell ores, buy new pickaxes, upgrade skills, and try to uncover all the secrets! Will you be able to challenge the dragon and earn the maximum coins? Or maybe luck from the lucky block will smile upon you?

Instructions

Your goal is to find all your friends and save them!

Complete tasks to progress through the story and unlock new opportunities.

Controls:

For Mobile:

Left joystick - Movement
Right joystick - Destroy blocks
Tap on the screen in an empty space - Place a block
For PC:

WASD/Arrow keys - Movement
Left Mouse Button - Destroy blocks
Right Mouse Button - Place a block
Hold Shift - Work with the background layer
E - Inventory
F - Open shop
Q - Drop item
ESC - Pause
