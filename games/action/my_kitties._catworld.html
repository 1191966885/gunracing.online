Game Description

Create your own world with unusual kittens! At the beginning of the game three kittens are waiting for you, with black, white and ginger colors. Create unprecedented colors, mix colors and get new ones: blue, pink, neon-green and others. All this is in our game! Replenish your collection and perform tasks!

Instructions

- Place 2 kitties in the house.
- New kittens will come out of the house.
- Place kitties with different colors in the house to get new unusual colors.
- Move the kitty to the bottom of the screen and it will turn into a card.
- You can save a card by placing it in the collection.
- Fill the pages with cards by completing tasks and opening new pages of the collection.
- If the kitties are not happy, place the extra ones in the collection or drag and drop them on the delete button.
