Game Description

Match Masters is a real-time multiplayer match-3 puzzle game where players compete head-to-head on the same board. Each move requires strategic thinking, as it can benefit both you and your opponent. Utilize unique boosters, participate in tournaments, and climb the global leaderboards in this dynamic and engaging game.

Instructions

Match 3 or more tiles of the same color to score points.
Matching blue stars charges your booster; red circles charge your opponent's.
Activate your booster strategically to gain an advantage.
Each player takes turns, making every move crucial.
