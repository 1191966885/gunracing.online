Game Description

Find objects on colorful maps with links and Easter eggs! An interesting puzzle casual indie game in the genres of "Hidden Objects" and "Find the Differences"! Embark on an exciting journey through beautiful 2D locations, where hundreds of hidden objects and riddles await you! Explore picturesque landscapes, mysterious cities and magical worlds to find all the hidden objects. And in the "Spot the Difference" mode, test your attentiveness by comparing two almost identical pictures!

Instructions

Control on the PC by clicking on the mouse.Click on the objects on the map, as in the lower part of the panel.

Moving around the map: By holding down the right mouse button or the arrow key on the keyboard

Zooming: Mouse wheel (scroll)

Control on a mobile device by tapping your finger on the screen.

Your task is to find all the items in the list.
Use the hints to find those items that are hidden too well :)
