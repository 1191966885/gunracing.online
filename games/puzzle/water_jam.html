Game Description

Water Jam is an addictive color-sorting puzzle game that challenges your strategy and problem-solving skills. Your task is simple: pour and sort colored water into the correct tubes, ensuring that each color matches perfectly. As you advance through levels, the puzzles become more complex, requiring you to think ahead and plan each move carefully. Navigate vibrant tubes, tap to pour, and use small storage cups to temporarily hold excess liquid, helping you clear tubes efficiently. With its stunning visuals, smooth controls, and progressively challenging levels, Water Jam offers a fresh take on color-sorting games that keep your mind sharp and entertained.
