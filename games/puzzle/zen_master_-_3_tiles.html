Zen Master - 3 tiles

Strategy Match-3 Brain

Game Description

You can test your skills in this addictive puzzle game without time or place constraints. If you like solving problems like Match 3, Sudoku or Mahjong, then you will definitely like this game. Collect three identical tiles to clear the board. Your goal is to completely clear the board of a variety of picture tiles. The levels start out easy and relaxing, but gradually become more challenging and addictive!

Instructions

Select three identical tiles to remove them from the board. You can only select tiles that are free and not covered from above. Only 8 tiles can fit in the bottom bar. If it fills up, you lose.

Your goal is to gradually remove all the tiles from the board. Use boosters to speed up the game.

Play Zen Master on different devices: computer, mobile phone, tablet.
